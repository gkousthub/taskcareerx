Margherita Pizza Recipe

This classic Margherita Pizza is a timeless favorite, featuring a simple yet delicious combination of tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and olive oil on a thin crust. It's the perfect dish for pizza lovers everywhere!

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional fillings: cheese, origano

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Transfer to Baking Surface: Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or lightly greased baking sheet
  5. Add Sauce: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  6. Arrange Cheese: Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  7. Add Basil Leaves: Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese.
  8. Drizzle Olive Oil: Drizzle a little extra virgin olive oil over the pizza, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Bake the Pizza: Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven or pizza stone. Bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Cool and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Margherita Pizza!
